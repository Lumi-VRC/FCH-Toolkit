FAQ

Q: What is FCH Toolkit?

A: FCH Toolkit is a web-based control panel designed to simplify VRChat community moderation and management.

Q: Why use Toolkit?

A: Toolkit focuses on safety, efficiency, and ease-of-use. It offers a great UI, easy access, dedicated Discord/API integration, and does not require unsafe permissions.

Q: What rules apply to my community?

A: There are no strict rules imposedâ€”just common sense. Use the tool responsibly and do not employ it maliciously.

Q: How do I add Toolkit to my group?

A: Your group must have over 200 users and pass a lightweight audit to ensure it's not malicious. Make an account and then contact me on Discord @ "lumi_vrc" to begin the process.

Q: What does the moderator workflow look like?

A: It resembles workflows in similar tools: moderate a user, log evidence in an automatically opened thread, tag the case, then close the thread to save it to your group's database.

Q: Can I view evidence logged in Toolkit as a group administrator?

A: Yes, Group Owners can designate other accounts as "Admins" to grant auditing permissions. Evidence, including videos, is visible as downloadable embeds on the website.

Features

VRChat / Discord Integration

When a user is warned/kicked/banned, a thread is automatically opened in your Discord.

In-App Moderation Auditing

View and sort moderation history for your group.

Embedded Evidence Viewing

Open and display uploaded evidence for each moderation event in-app.

Enhanced Group Management

Ban and Unban directly from the app! (Optional)

Moderator Activity Tracking

Sort moderation events by moderator to help manage team activity.

Moderation Analytics

Easy-to-understand data points about your group's moderation history.

Long-term Evidence Storage

Evidence is stored via Amazon AWS S3 and moved to AWS Glacier after two months.
